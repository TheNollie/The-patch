Opinion pieces don't need a score or a pros/cons list — just your `category: opinions` in the front matter and you're set. Lead with your actual argument in the first paragraph; don't bury it.

## A simple structure

- **The claim.** State your take plainly in the first two sentences.
- **The evidence.** Two or three concrete examples that back it up.
- **The counterpoint.** Acknowledge the strongest disagreement, then explain why you still land where you do.
- **The button.** One closing line that's quotable — this is often what gets clipped for TikTok or X.
