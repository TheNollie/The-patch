Welcome to **The Patch** — a gaming site built around news, reviews, and opinion pieces, delivered fast and without the fluff. Every post here gets a version tag, just like a real patch note, because that's basically what this site is: a running changelog of everything happening in gaming.

## What you'll find here

- **News** — announcements, release dates, and the stuff that just dropped
- **Reviews** — full write-ups with a Verdict Patch scorecard at the end
- **Opinions** — columns, hot takes, and analysis

## How to write a new post

If you're the site owner reading this in the admin panel: every article is just a Markdown file with a few fields at the top (the "front matter") — title, category, date, and so on. Fill those in, write your article below in plain text, hit publish, and Netlify rebuilds the site automatically. No coding required.

Check the **README** in the project for the full walkthrough.
